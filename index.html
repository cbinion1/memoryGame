Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped over, face up each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
